🎲 D&D Dice Roller

A realistic 3D dice simulator for Dungeons & Dragons tabletop RPGs

Basic Dice Rolling

Use the + and − buttons under each die face (d4, d6, d8, d10, d12, d20) to set how many of each die you want to throw. Press Roll to launch them. The total and individual results appear in the floating panel at the top of the board.

Press Clear to remove all dice from the board and reset every counter and the modifier back to zero.

Modifier

Use the Mod. control in the bottom row to add a positive or negative integer to your total. The modifier is shown in the result breakdown and applied to the final sum.

Advantage & Disadvantage

The Advantage and Disadvantage buttons instantly roll two d20 dice, ignoring whatever die counts you have set.

  • Advantage: the higher of the two values counts toward the total.
  • Disadvantage: the lower of the two values counts toward the total.
The same options are also available when rolling from the Skills & Proficiencies panel.

d100 – Percentile Roll

Tap d100 to make a percentile roll. Two d10 dice are thrown: the first represents the tens digit (00, 10, 20 … 90) and the second the units digit (0–9). Results range from 1 to 100. A result of double-zero (00 + 0) counts as 100.

Natural 1 & Natural 20

When a single d20 is rolled alone, or an Advantage/Disadvantage throw is rolled (2d20), a result of 1 or 20 shows a toast on screen:

  • Nat. 1 – automatic failure.
  • Nat. 20 – critical or automatic success.

The toast dismisses automatically after a few seconds.

Character Sheet 👤

Tap 👤 to open the Character Sheet panel. It has four sections:

Name, Level & HP – Set your character name, level and HP/HP max.

Basic Stats – change values with +/−

  • AC: Set your AC
  • Initiative: Set your Initiative, touch value to roll a d20 and apply mod.
  • Speed: Set your speed, touch "Speed" label to change type (Speed, Fly, Climb, Swim and Burrow).
  • Passive Perception: Set your PP.
  • Prof. Bonus: Set your PB, this value is added to your Saving Throws and your Skills mod.
  • Hit Dice: Set your Hit Dice type (d6, d8, d10, d12) and the ammount of dices you have (max. ammount equal to your level). Touch the value to roll one dice and add result to your HP.

Abilities – Set your six ability scores (STR, DEX, CON, INT, WIS, CHA) with the +/− buttons. Modifiers are calculated and displayed automatically.

Saving Throws – Tap the modifier button next to each ability to roll a saving throw directly. Enable the checkbox below it to add your Proficiency Bonus.

Skills – Tap a skill name to roll a check with the correct ability modifier. Use the toggle button on the right to cycle proficiency:

• No proficiency
P Proficient
E Expertise
All skills, saving throws and initiative rolls open a picker so you can choose Normal, Advantage or Disadvantage before the dice are thrown.

Roll History 📋

A simple and intuitive registry that shows your last 10 rolls. Record rolls of all types with the Total and the Details, such as the number of dice and the type of roll you made.

Custom Rolls 🎲

Tap 🎲 to manage saved roll presets. Give it a name, choose any combination of dice and an optional modifier, then tap + Add Roll to save it. Saved presets appear in a list — tap 🎲 to launch one instantly, or ✕ to delete it.

Dice Color 🎨

Tap 🎨 to change the look of your dice. Pick a custom color with the colour picker, or choose one of the Gem Themes — special textures that replace the solid colour:

  • Amber, Amethyst, Aquamarine, Diamond, Emerald, Onyx, Ruby, Sapphire.

Physics Settings ⚙️

Tap ⚙️ to adjust the physics simulation. Each card cycles through its options on tap:

📏 SizeSmall | Medium | Large — changes the physical scale of every die.
🌑 GravityZero | Soft | Normal — how fast the dice fall to the surface.
🥌 FrictionOff | On — controls how much the surface grips the dice.
🏀 BounceOff | Normal | High — controls how energetically the dice rebound.
💡 LightDim | Bright | Intense — ambient light intensity in the scene.
🔲 ShadowOff | On — toggles cast shadows on the surface.
Physics changes take effect when you close the panel. The dice engine restarts automatically.

Saved State

All your preferences are saved automatically in your browser's local storage: die counts, modifier, ability scores, proficiencies, custom roll presets, dice colour, and physics settings. Everything is restored the next time you open the app.

Saving Character and Custom Rolls

You can save your Character Sheet and Custom Rolls data. To do so, open 👤 Character Sheet and use 💾 / 📁 buttons to export/import data. A JSON file will be automatically saved to your Downloads folder, which you can later load to recover all data in case you lose or accidentally modify it.
